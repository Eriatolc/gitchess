GIT CHESS

Welcome

Git Chess is a system for playing chess via the Git version control system.

While Git was originally created for developing and collaborating on software, it also works well for collaborating on games of chess. Rather than making and committing software features, you make and commit chess moves. Your opponent pulls your changes, and then makes their move.

Getting Started

Do these things, or do your own thing:

  1. Fork/download/whatever the repo here.
  2. Follow the instructions on how to make a move. It's easy. When you're done, push your changes.
  3. Get a friend to clone or fork the repo, and wait for them to make their move.

What Else?

While Git Chess is a fun, delightfully inefficient way to play a game of chess, it is also an open source project. If you inspired, during the course of a game, to make improvements, artistic additions, or other modifications, just include them in your commit. Feel free to share any of these modifications or improvements by submitting a pull request or contacting Git Chess.
